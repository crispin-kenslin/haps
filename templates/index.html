Herbicidal Activity Prediction System using Machine Learning

Herbicides are used for selective killing of weeds in crop fields.

Predicting the herbicidal activity of chemical compounds is an important task in agrochemical discovery and development.

This system predicts herbicidal activity of chemical compounds using MACCS structural fingerprints and Support Vector Machine (SVM)

The predictions are explained by the contribution of top 20 MACCS structural fingerprints to the prediction.
